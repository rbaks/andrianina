## The open source community

The open-source encapsulates the idea behind a powerful community that can break barriers made by misleading governments in developing countries. Governments took their supremacy from the people, so why not give that power back to the people? at least hypothetically. This is where the open source community really shines.

We know DeFi projects or [Decentralized Finance](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Decentralized_finance) that stops Banks from controlling your money thanks to the [blockchain](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Blockchain) and [cryptocurrencies](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Cryptocurrency). It assures total transparency of transactions and is the most secure way to do so, at least at this time being.

On the other hand, we have decentralized Web or [Web 3.0](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Web3) that erradicates any form of content censorhip on the internet. That may be any form of political contestation

In the financial industry, This power currently knows its bests in the financial industry, thanks to the blockchain and cryptocurrencies. I am totally convinced that only communities and entrepreneurs can lead a country to truly sustainable development by leveraging open source projects.
